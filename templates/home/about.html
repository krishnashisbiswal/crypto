About AE Platform

Transforming global crude oil trading through blockchain innovation, transparency, and trust.

Our Mission

AE is on a mission to transform global crude oil trading by harnessing blockchain's power to bring unprecedented transparency, efficiency and trust to the energy markets.

We tokenize physical oil barrels into digital assets, unlocking new liquidity and automating the trade lifecycle to eliminate legacy delays. Our platform creates a single, immutable ledger for every transaction, setting a new standard for secure, real-time trade execution.

Our Vision

The vision driving AE is a future where oil – and ultimately all energy commodities – are traded seamlessly on a decentralized network.

By breaking down geographical and bureaucratic barriers, we aim to democratize access to the oil market worldwide, creating a more inclusive and efficient energy economy.

The Challenge in Oil Trading

Traditional crude-oil trading remains paper-driven and fragmented, creating inefficiencies that cost the industry billions annually.

Slow Settlements

Oil trades often take days or weeks to settle, bogged down by paper contracts, manual verification and multiple intermediaries.

Opaque Processes

Siloed data and fragmented information systems raise operational costs and expose trades to fraud and disputes.

High Fees

The $2 trillion oil market suffers from high fees and information asymmetry, making fair pricing and fast settlement difficult.
