The Revolution in Oil Trading

AE is reimagining global crude oil markets for the digital age, replacing centuries-old paper-based processes with blockchain-powered automation.

Legacy Market Challenges

For decades, oil trading has been bound by inefficient processes that cost the industry billions annually.

Slow, Fragmented Settlements

Oil trades typically require extensive paperwork and intermediaries, stretching settlement across days or weeks. This lag ties up capital and creates counterparty risk on large transactions.

Opaque Transparency

Each trading party keeps separate records, making it hard to verify volumes or provenance. Fragmented ledgers open the door to errors or fraud, eroding trust between stakeholders.

High Costs

Cumbersome post-trade processing inflates costs. Industry pilots estimate blockchain platforms can cut back-office expenses by roughly 40%.

Geopolitical Fragmentation

Multiple exchanges and jurisdictions require separate KYC and compliance, sidelining smaller players and limiting liquidity access.

Compliance Burdens

Tracking everything from contracts to carbon emissions is laborious. Firms juggle audits across disparate databases, slowing operations.

AE's Blockchain Solutions

AE deploys a suite of blockchain-powered tools to tackle these challenges head-on.

Tokenization of Oil Barrels

Every physical barrel in AE's network is represented by a 1:1 crypto token on-chain. These "digital twins" are backed by audited storage facilities, making oil instantly fungible and enabling fractional ownership.

Learn More

Real-Time Traceability

AE's distributed ledger logs every transfer of tokenized oil. Participants can trace a barrel's origin, custody and quality in real time. Immutability ensures data cannot be altered.

Explore

Smart-Contract Settlements

Self-executing contracts automatically handle payment and title transfer. Once pre-defined conditions are met, funds and oil tokens exchange hands instantly, removing counterparty risk.
