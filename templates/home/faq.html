About AE and Tokenization

What is AE?

AE is a blockchain-based platform that tokenizes crude oil, enabling secure, transparent, and real-time trading of oil-backed digital assets.

AE introduces a dual-token system:

  • AE utility token - for governance, access, staking
  • AE-OIL token - representing physical oil reserves

How does AE-OIL work?

AE-OIL is an asset-backed token corresponding to a specific quantity of crude oil. Each token is linked to verifiable reserves stored in third-party custody and can be traded or redeemed.

Key features:

  • 1:1 backing with physical oil barrels
  • Embedded metadata (grade, location, ESG data)
  • Real-time tracking and verification
  • Redeemable for physical delivery

Is AE a cryptocurrency or a commodity token?

AE includes both:

  • AE Utility Token - for governance, access, staking
  • AE-OIL Token - representing physical oil assets

This dual structure provides both utility functions and real-world asset backing.

Technical & Blockchain

What blockchain is AE built on?

AE is built on Ethereum and optionally runs on Layer-2 solutions for better scalability and lower gas fees.

Supported networks include:

  • Ethereum mainnet
  • Polygon for lower fees
  • Arbitrum for fast transactions
  • Custom sidechains for enterprise use

What ensures the security of the platform?

Multiple layers of security protect the platform:

  • Audited smart contracts by top-tier firms
  • Multi-signature wallets
  • Secure APIs and infrastructure
  • Regular penetration testing
  • Cold storage for large holdings
  • Insurance coverage

How is custody of oil reserves verified?

Through licensed storage facilities and blockchain-based oracles that report real-time reserve data.

Verification process:

  • Third-party audited storage facilities
  • Regular inventory certifications
  • Blockchain oracles for real-time data
  • Transparent reserve reporting
  • Public attestations

Regulatory & Legal

Is AE legal in my country?

AE complies with international standards, but some regions may restrict access. Please consult local laws.

We operate in compliance with:

  • Financial regulations in major jurisdictions
  • Anti-money laundering (AML) laws
  • Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements
  • Commodity trading regulations

Do I need to complete KYC to use AE?

Yes. KYC is mandatory to access AE trading, redemption, and utility features.

KYC requirements include:

  • Government-issued ID verification
  • Proof of address
  • Selfie verification
  • Source of funds declaration (for large amounts)

Are there any deposit or withdrawal fees?

We charge minimal platform fees for trading and settlement. Deposit fees vary by payment method.

Fee structure:

  • Trading fees: 0.1% - 0.25%
  • Deposit fees: Free for crypto, 2-3% for cards
  • Withdrawal fees: Network gas fees apply
  • Redemption fees: Logistics and processing costs

All fees are transparently displayed before confirming transactions.
