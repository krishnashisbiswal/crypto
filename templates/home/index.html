How do I learn how to trade?

Start with our demo account to practice trading without risk. We also provide educational resources, tutorials, and market analysis to help you understand oil trading and blockchain technology.

How long does it take to withdraw funds?

Withdrawals are processed instantly through smart contracts. Fiat withdrawals typically take 1-3 business days depending on your payment method and bank processing times.

What is trading platform and what it is for?

AE is a blockchain-based platform for trading tokenized crude oil. It allows you to buy, sell, and trade oil-backed digital assets with transparency, instant settlement, and global accessibility.

Yes! Our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices. You can trade, monitor your portfolio, and access all platform features on your mobile device.

The minimum deposit amount is $10 USD. This low barrier to entry makes AE accessible to traders of all levels and investment capacities.

We charge minimal platform fees for trading and settlement. Deposit fees vary by payment method. All fees are transparently displayed before confirming any transaction.
